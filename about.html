About Me

Hi, I'm Anson — a designer and front-end developer based in Vancouver, with a background in Interactive Arts & Technology from Simon Fraser University. I specialize in UI/UX design, digital production, and front-end development, with a focus on creating clean, scalable experiences across web platforms.

My approach is grounded in design systems thinking, layout precision, and a strong eye for how visual design meets functionality. Whether it's shipping production-ready landing pages or contributing to internal tools and design ops, I enjoy bringing structure and polish to every stage of a digital product.

I've previously worked on branded site templates at PressReader and supported UX and visual QA efforts on enterprise platforms at ICBC. These experiences helped me build a workflow that’s collaborative, detail-focused, and comfortable working across design and development teams.

I’m currently open to junior and entry-level opportunities in UI/UX design, digital production, or front-end development. I’m especially drawn to teams that value thoughtful design execution, clear communication, and well-crafted digital experiences.
